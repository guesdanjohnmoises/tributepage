Manny Pacquiao: The People's Champ

Manny Pacquiao — World Boxing Champion, Senator, and Filipino icon of perseverance and humility.

Emmanuel Dapidran Pacquiao, known globally as Manny “Pacman” Pacquiao, was born on December 17, 1978 in Kibawe, Bukidnon, Philippines. He rose from humble beginnings to become the only boxer in history to win world titles in eight different weight divisions. Beyond boxing, Pacquiao has served as a congressman and senator, dedicating his life to public service and helping the poor.

His story continues to inspire millions around the world — a true symbol of discipline, faith, and hard work.
