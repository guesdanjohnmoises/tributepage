Manny Pacquiao: The People's Champ

About Manny Pacquiao

Manny "Pacman" Pacquiao is a Filipino professional boxer and politician. Known for his speed, power, and relentless fighting style, he is the only boxer in history to win world titles in 8 different weight divisions.

Career Highlights

  • First and only eight-division world champion
  • 12 major world titles won
  • Named Fighter of the Decade by BWAA, WBO, and WBC
  • Ranked among the greatest boxers of all time

Legacy

Beyond boxing, Pacquiao is known for his public service, humanitarian work, and his inspiring story of rising from poverty to becoming a global icon.
