Manny Pacquiao: The People's Champ

About Manny Pacquiao

Manny "Pacman" Pacquiao is a Filipino professional boxer and politician. Known for his speed, power, and relentless fighting style, Pacquiao's name is forever written in boxing history as the only boxer to win world titles in **eight weight divisions**.

Career Highlights

  • First and only eight-division world champion
  • 12 major world titles
  • Named "Fighter of the Decade" by WBO, WBC, and WBA
  • Ranked among the greatest boxers of all time

Legacy

Beyond boxing, Pacquiao is known for his public service, humanitarian work, and his inspiring story of rising from poverty to becoming a global icon.
