Francis Gatilowan: the one and only man's not hot

Manny Pacquiao — World Boxing Champion, Senator, and Filipino icon of perseverance and humility.

Francis Gatilowan was born on December 17, 1978, in Bauko, Mountain Province, Philippines. He rose from humble beginnings to become a professional IT Developer, and he dreams of becoming a congressman to help poor communities and uplift lives through technology and public service.

His dream school has always been the University of the Cordilleras (UC), where he hopes to further sharpen his skills and prove that success is possible for anyone who works hard and believes in their purpose.

His story continues to inspire me as a student—to aim higher, work harder, and never forget where I came from.
